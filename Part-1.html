Definition( Semi Simple Group): A connected linear Algebraic Group GGG over an algebraically closed field is called semi simple if every smooth connected solvable normal subgroup of GGG is trivial.

Definition (Reductive group): A connected linear algebraic group GGG over an algebraically closed field is reductive if every smooth connected unipotent normal subgroup of GGG is trivial.

Definition (Maximal compact subgroup) A maximal compact subgroup KKK of topological group GGG is a subgroup KKK that is compact space in subspace topology and maximal amongst such subgroups.
If GGG is a connected LIe group, then any compact subgroup of GGG is contained is contained in some maximal compact subgroup (in particular maximal compact subgroup must exists) and all maximal compact subgroups of GGG are connected and conjugate to each other.
