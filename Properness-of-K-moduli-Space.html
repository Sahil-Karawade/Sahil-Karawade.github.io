Properness of the K-moduli space

These notes were taken during talk given by Ziquan Zhuang (MIT)

The main aim of this talk: Is there a “nice” moduli space of Fano varieties.

Nice means separated, proper and even projective &
Fano means complete variety who’s anticanonical bundle Kx∗K_x^*Kx∗​ is ample.

Yau-Tian-Donaldson conjecture:
  • For XXX=Fano variety. Existence of Ka¨\ddot{a}a¨hler-Einstein (KE) metric is equivalent to XXX is k−k-k−polystable (algebraic stability condition).

This motivates the following conjecture:

k−k-k−Moduli conjecture:
  • There exists proper moduli space of k−k-k−polystable Fano varieties and all connected components are projective.
